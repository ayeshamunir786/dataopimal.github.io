A gerund is a verbal that ends in -ing and functions as a noun.
The term verbal indicates that a gerund, like the other two kinds of verbals,
is based on a verb and therefore expresses action or a state of being. However,
since a gerund functions as a noun, it occupies some positions in a sentence tha
t a noun ordinarily would, for example: subject, direct object, subject complement,
and object of preposition.
